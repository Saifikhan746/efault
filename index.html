Dear Visitor,

I'm Efault. I look at systems from the inside — from the broken parts, from the places nobody thought to lock. Security is my focus: reverse engineering, binary exploitation, web vulnerabilities, OSINT. Not to cause damage, but because understanding how something fails is the only honest way to know how it works.

Privacy is a principle, not a setting. I back the projects that treat it that way. Professionally I'm headed into Dev and DevSecOps — shipping security with the code, not as an afterthought. The rest of the time goes to CTFs, disassembly, and whatever cipher refuses to make sense. This site is where I write it all down.

"We're thieves, in a world that don't want us no more."

— Arthur Morgan
